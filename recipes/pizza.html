Thin Crust Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Lukewarm water
  • Flour
  • Salt

How to Make Thin Crust Pizza Dough

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle

How to Use Thin Curst Pizza Dough

Top the curst as desired and bake at 500ºF (260ºC) until golden, for 8 to 10 minnutes.
